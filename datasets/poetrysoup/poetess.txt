
ANDREA DIETRICH




Amble now, down refined erudite avenues; delight in elegant tableaux, rich in colorful hues.



(How I feel when I read Andrea’s poems) One Liner Acrostic with Internal Rhyme
Contest: Let’s Talk About Andrea Dietrich One Liner
Sponsor: Silent One
6th October 2016


Amble:  Walk at a slow, relaxed pace, especially for pleasure
Refined:  Elegant and cultured in appearance, manner, or taste
Erudite:  showing great knowledge
Tableaux:  A group of models/pictures representing a scene from a story
