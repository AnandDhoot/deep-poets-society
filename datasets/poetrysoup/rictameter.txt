
Clowns

Scared? Scarred? 
Clowns joyous or-
Creepy, crawling killers.
Stalking  younglings,  watching   we  humans 
Instant   scared!  Sights unexpected  - obscene 
Scarred a  lifetime   with   undue   fears  
Parties  stress   throughout   years  
Tough task  awaits-
lights out. 



Masked clown sightings have now invaded my hometown.
