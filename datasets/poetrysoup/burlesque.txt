GRAMMATICAL   CLARIFICTION


For grammarians,
When they say
It’s “it’s”,  it’s  “it’s”
And when they say 
It’s  “its”, it’s “its”


But non-grammarians
Often say it’s “it’s”
But it’s really “its”
And vice versa.
