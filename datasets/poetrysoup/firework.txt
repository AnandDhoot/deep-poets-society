
- Fanfare - Pleiades F -



Fall are glowing of gold

Flooding of nice colors  

Flutter neurotic leaves 
 
Fruitful trees with apples 

Frost on the creek at night 
 
Foretaste of the winter  

Flashbacks ... summer pleasures
